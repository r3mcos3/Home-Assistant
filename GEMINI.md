### Entity Naming Convention (for Home Assistant entities)
A standardized English naming schema is used for consistency: `{floor}_{room}_{function}_{device_type}`.

*   **Floor Prefixes:** `gf_` (Ground Floor), `ff_` (First Floor), `bf_` (Basement), `at_` (Attic), `out_` (Outside).
*   **Device Type Suffixes:** `light`, `spots`, `ceiling`, `motion`, `contact`, `switch`.
*   **Room Names:** `kitchen`, `living`, `bedroom_main`, `bedroom_elyza`, `bedroom_santi`, `toilet`, `hallway`, `landing`, `basement`, `attic`, `shed`, `conservatory`, `garden`.
*   **Examples:** `light.gf_kitchen_light`, `binary_sensor.bf_basement_motion_occupancy`.
*   **Multiple Devices:** Use numbered suffixes (e.g., `light.gf_living_ceiling_1`).
*   **Seasonal Devices:** Format: `{floor}_{room}_{season}_{item}` (e.g., `light.gf_living_christmas_tree`).
